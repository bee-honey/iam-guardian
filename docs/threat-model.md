# Threat Model

IAM Guardian focuses on identity-centric security risks.

## Threats

### Excessive Privilege

Users or service accounts possess permissions beyond operational requirements.

### Privilege Escalation

Permissions inherited through groups, composite roles, or service accounts lead to unintended administrative access.

### Lateral Movement

Compromised identities can access additional resources through inherited permissions.

### Service Account Abuse

Long-lived service accounts accumulate excessive permissions over time.

## Security Principles

### Deterministic Analysis

Security findings are generated by deterministic graph analysis.

AI-generated content never determines risk severity.

### Explainability

Every finding must include a verifiable privilege path.

### Least Privilege

Recommendations should reduce permissions while maintaining functionality.

### Human Approval

Future remediation workflows require explicit human approval before changes are applied to Keycloak.
